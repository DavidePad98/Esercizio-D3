Focaccia with Caramelized Onins, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!"

Image:Sally Vegas

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

    For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 onces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flor is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I Simetimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour
